University Overview & Contact Information

Chitkara University is a government-recognized private university established under The Chitkara University Act by the Punjab State Legislature. It is authorized to award degrees under Sections 2(f) and 22(1) of the UGC Act, 1956, and is recognized among North India’s leading private universities for its industry-integrated programs and global academic collaborations. The university’s vision is to achieve global recognition in promoting academic excellence through interdisciplinary research and innovation. Its mission is to align educational practices with international standards, nurture entrepreneurship and research partnerships globally, and instill strong ethical and professional values among students. Chitkara University operates two main campuses — one in Punjab, located on the Chandigarh–Patiala National Highway, and another in Himachal Pradesh, situated along the Pinjore–Barotiwala National Highway. For admissions and inquiries, the contact helplines are Chandigarh (95011 05714, 95011 05715), Delhi/NCR (95993 68734), and WhatsApp (98590 00000). The university also offers a missed-call service at 1800 267 1999 and can be contacted via email at admissions@chitkara.edu.in



General Admission Information

Admission to all undergraduate programs requires successful completion of Class XII from a recognized board with the specified minimum percentage, depending on the course. Engineering admissions are based on JEE Mains 2025 (January or April sessions), and candidates must have been born on or after October 1, 2003. All B.E. students must possess a personal laptop. Applicants are required to submit their Class X and XII mark sheets, migration certificate, character certificate, Aadhaar card, medical fitness certificate, and, where applicable, the entrance exam rank card. The first-year fee includes a one-time ₹20,000 charge covering Educational Technology Services (ETS) and Career Advancement Services (CAS). Fees are subject to revision by the university. Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance to appear for final examinations. Seat reservations are provided as follows: Punjab campus reserves 15% of seats for Punjab domicile students, while Himachal Pradesh campus reserves 25% for HP domicile students. Reservations for SC/ST/OBC and other categories follow the respective state government policies.

Business and Management Programs

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is offered in multiple specializations including Professional, Retail, Logistics, FinTech & AI, Data Science & AI, Digital Transformation, and Aviation Management. Each of these programs runs for three years, with an optional fourth-year Honours extension.
BBA in Professional

The Bachelor of Business Administration (Professional) program provides a comprehensive foundation in core business disciplines such as marketing, finance, human resources, and operations. It emphasizes practical exposure, case-based learning, and industry integration to develop strong managerial and entrepreneurial skills.
Duration: 3 years (optional 4th-year Honours)
Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream with a minimum of 50% marks, followed by an interview.
Fees: ₹70,000 for the first year and ₹77,000 per semester thereafter, including Honours semesters.

Campus Facilities and Student Life

Chitkara University offers advanced infrastructure and facilities to ensure an enriching academic and personal experience. The university provides high-tech classrooms, digital libraries with access to databases like Science Direct and EBSCO, and modern laboratories for all disciplines. The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled. The hostel facilities are secure and segregated for boys and girls, offering comfortable accommodation, common rooms, TV lounges, and high-speed internet. The university also provides an extensive range of sports facilities, including cricket, football, basketball, tennis, badminton, and swimming pool facilities, along with fully equipped gyms. A 24-hour medical centre ensures round-the-clock healthcare, with all students covered under a comprehensive health insurance policy. Additional amenities include on-campus cafeterias, auditoriums, transport connectivity to nearby cities, and banking services with a UCO Bank counter and a 24/7 ATM.

Placements and Industry Collaboration

Chitkara University is known for its high placement records and deep industry integration. The university reports placement rates exceeding 90% in engineering and management programs. The 2023–24 academic year recorded a median salary of ₹9.72 LPA for B.Tech students and ₹11.5 LPA for MBA graduates. The highest packages for Computer Science graduates often exceed ₹40 LPA. The university maintains over 300 corporate partnerships with organizations such as Microsoft, Amazon, Deloitte, Infosys, Google, Wipro, Cognizant, Bosch, Siemens, EY, and Fortis. The Career Development Cell at Chitkara provides continuous support through resume workshops, interview training, mentorship, and internship placements to ensure students are fully industry-ready.
